Are you commercial, proactive and driven and do you want to work at a young and enterprising technical secondment agency? Then we would like to get in touch with you! We were founded 3 years ago and are growing rapidly. In the past year we have also been able to help many professionals find a job and we have started a collaboration with various new clients. To continue this growth in 2023, we are looking for internal reinforcement.

We are looking for a commercial and driven Industry Consultant for our growing office in Zaandam.

What are you going to do

As a Junior Industry Consultant you will be part of a small team of experienced consultants. We will help you build long-term relationships with technical companies, build a network with highly educated technical professionals and become a specialist in (technical) secondment. In short, an investment for the long term!

Your activities will include:

(Technical) companies:

  • Building your own customer portfolio through cold and warm acquisition;
  • Introducing our services (secondment) and making associated hiring agreements;
  • Discuss vacancies in detail in order to recruit the right professionals;
  • Maintaining, expanding and improving a long-term relationship with your clients;
  • Working together with your clients and contacts in a pleasant way!

(Technical) professionals:

  • Recruiting technical professionals by:o writing and publishing vacancies;o make approaches on LinkedIn, Indeed, Werk.nl, etc. with your vacancies;o visiting colleges, universities and fairs;
  • Conduct job interviews to map wishes, ambitions and terms of employment;
  • Making the right match between the wishes and ambitions of the technical professionals and vacancies or clients we work with;
  • Conducting employment conditions discussions to welcome new colleagues within Betaselect;
  • Conduct progress interviews with your seconded professionals to support them in their personal development;
  • Get to know your seconded colleagues better during employee outings;
  • Supporting them when they start working for our clients, job done!

This is where you end up

We are Betaselect, a technical secondment agency founded in 2020 with offices in Zaandam and Groningen. You will work at our office in Zaandam in the team together with Kim Oostendorp, Marnix Haitsma and Kevin Overtoom. We all have many years of experience in technical secondment and are working with great enthusiasm to put Betaselect on the map.

Together we will continue to build in 2023, with our ambition to quickly grow into a fully-fledged team. From our office in Zaandam we work in the provinces of North Holland, Flevoland, Utrecht and South Holland. Your clients will therefore mainly be located in this region, we recruit the technical professionals both nationally and worldwide!

When do you make the difference?

You are naturally interested in people and find it a challenge to bring supply and demand together. You will not find the sheep with the 5 legs. It is therefore up to you to make the best possible match based on skills, norms and values ​​and cognition.

You are a good match with us if you:

  • have completed a higher professional education in HRM, Commercial Economics or Communication;
  • are communicatively strong;
  • are commercial and enterprising;
  • are a proactive self-starter;
  • are confident and have a firm footing;
  • have already gained some experience within secondment / sales (NOT a must).

What do we have to offer you?

We offer you a job in which you can develop and become a specialist within technical secondment. You will learn a lot about the technology, the technical companies and get to know many technical professionals. In addition, we invest in you and ensure that you become well versed in our services, laws and regulations, safety and building relationships.

  • You will be offered a starting salary (full-time) between € 2,750 and € 3,500 gross per month, depending on the knowledge and work experience (0-3 years) you already bring;
  • A lease car is ready for you so that you have complete freedom to visit clients, and you can also use the car privately;
  • You will be offered 30 vacation days;
  • Betaselect provides a joint lunch every afternoon;
  • You will be offered a non-contributory pension;
  • Our working hours are flexible, we assume your sense of responsibility;
  • You will be working for a young organization with many challenges but also many opportunities;
  • Working out during office hours is something we do together every week;
  • We celebrate joint successes with joint outings.
